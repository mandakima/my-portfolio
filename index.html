About

Hey there! Get to know more about this accountant-turned-designer & developer! Professional summary to the left, and skills to the right.

Designer w/ Dev Chops 🦄

I'm a versatile creative developer based in Vancouver, Washington, who has built and designed a variety of awesome things at Silvertrek. These include an app for construction mechanics and equipment managers, a revamped website, and marketing collateral. With a background in UX, front-end development, and marketing, I bring a unique blend of creativity and technical expertise to every project.

I focus on practicality and feasibility to bring solutions to life, so I dabble a lot with front-end development & Figma. This unique super skill set allows me to collaborate easily with developers to ensure that we create is effectively shipped.

Overall, I'm always thinking about how to strategically improve designs and processes, helping businesses achieve their goals while prioritizing user experience.

On a personal note, some of my favorite hobbies include playing video games, reading psychology articles, and watching anime. While you're still here, check out my resume to see my accomplishments!

Kartaya Maintain

UX Design & Development

Construction mechanics and equipment managers face unique challenges daily, from manual work management to equipment maintenance.


Discover how I streamlined their workflows and improved efficiency with user-centered design solutions that address their daily frustrations, specifically tailored for Viewpoint users.


Note: This case study is under construction and pending approval for display without an NDA.


Restaurant Technologies

Web Design and Development

This massive web redesign project was initiated by the Web Development Manager, who crafted the initial look and feel. Afterwards, I collaborated with three other developers to implement designs for both cornerstone pages and their subpages.


Our focus was on enhancing the user experience for Restaurant Technologies, the leading provider of cooking oil management and clean hood system solutions to over 36,000 quick-service and full-service restaurant chains, independent restaurants, hotels, casinos, convenience stores, universities, and hospitals nationwide!

Energized Electric LLC

Web Design and Development

Energized Electric, a local electrical company specializing in old home rewires and restoration, collaborates with some of Portland's top remodelers and restorers.


I implemented the design created by the Senior Web Developer and made slight visual adjustments after discussion. Following the handoff, I also managed ongoing implementation based on client requests, including creating additional pages and making further visual changes.

Grove at 162nd

Web Design

ZenithPro acquired the Grove at 162nd, a community of 26 detached, single-family homes, from another property management group. To showcase this rental property, I gathered ZenithPro's business requirements and designed the website.


After launching the initial site, it was handed over to a Junior Developer for ongoing updates and implementation based on client requests, such as adding images or additional content.


Beela (Phase 4)

Web Development

Beela is an inclusive Swedish nonprofit organization that supports women and nonbinary individuals pursuing their careers in technology. Our group of 5 developers collaborated with 5 designers and researchers to create a responsive website using SCSS, React.JS, and Typescript.
