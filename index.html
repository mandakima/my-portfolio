Projects // Client Works

Here are some examples of recent projects I've worked on! If you're interested in seeing more of my work, feel free to send me a message via my e-mail!

Restaurant Technologies

Web Design and Development

Restaurant Technologies is the leading provider of cooking oil management and clean hood system solutions to more than 36,000 quick-service and full-service restaurant chains, independent restaurants, hotels, casinos, convenience stores, universities, and hospitals nationwide.


This was a massive web redesign project, with the initial design created by the Web Development Manager during Phase 1. I took part in Phase 2 with three other developers and implemented designs for both cornerstone pages and its subpages, particularly depot locations and case studies.

Energized Electric LLC

Web Design and Development

Energized Electric is a young and local electrical company that collaborates with some of Portland's top remodelers and restorers.


I took charge of Phase 2, where I implemented the design created by the Senior Web Developer in Phase 1. Subsequently, Phase 3 was handed over to the Junior Developer for ongoing implementation, driven by the client's requests.

Grove at 162nd

Web Design and Development

The Grove at 162nd is a community of 26 detached, single-family homes that aims to fulfill attainable housing options for working families and first-time home buyers.


I took charge of Phase 1 and Phase 2, where I designed and implemented the specific requirements of Zenith Pro for their new, modern rental property. Subsequently, Phase 3 was passed on to the Junior Developer for continuous implementation based on the client's requests.

Beela (Phase 4)

Web Development

Our group of 5 developers collaborated with 5 designers and researchers to create a responsive website using SCSS, React.JS, and Typescript for Beela, a Swedish nonprofit organization that supports women and nonbinary individuals pursuing careers in technology.


The website with our implemented design went live on January 2023!

Develop for Good

UX Design & Visual Branding

Develop for Good is a nonprofit design agency that specializes in building products for other nonprofits. Through our work, student volunteers gain valuable technical experience, while nonprofits receive high-quality digital services.


I had the opportunity to collaborate with two designers on the visual rebranding project for Develop for Good, working within a lean UX environment.
