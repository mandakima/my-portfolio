About Me

I'm a second-generation Cambodian American and the first in my family to graduate from college. I was an accounting professional who have worked for some Fortune 500 companies, such as Vail Resorts and Marriott International. Now, I have goals to become a UI Developer and my journey has been challenging, yet rewarding! How did it all began? It all started with Neopets & Geocities ...

I'm on the journey to become a UI Developer to conceptualize and design things on the internet! I have completed a rigorous UX Design education through Designlab and have mentors from Google & Airbnb to teach me on what it means to be a designer. Since May 2022, I'm currently enrolled in Skillspire a local bootcamp in the beautiful PNW with a mission to empower under-represented population. I'm expecting to complete the full-stack web development (MERN) immersive program on September 9, 2022!

While I'm not coding or designing, you can find me playing video games, doodling, learning how to make vegan sweets, and reading about human psychology.

TL;DR: I'm a previous accounting professional pivoting into UI Development! I took a UX program to understand the design thinking process and gain a deeper understanding empathy. I'm taking a full-stack development to be a developer with a empathy-focused approach. I like video games, baking vegan sweets, and reading human psychology.
