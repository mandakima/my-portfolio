About Me

TL;DR: I'm a previous accounting professional pivoting into UI Development.

Hey there!

I'm a second-generation Cambodian American and the first in my family to graduate from college. Previously, I was an accountant employed by Fortune 500 companies, including Vail Resorts and Marriott International. It wasn't long before I discovered I didn't just want to help business needs, but also create solutions for users.

As a UI Developer, I am driven to find effective solutions and bring a realistic outlook to every collaborative environment. Designlab taught me how to think like a designer with users in mind, and I'm currently studying web development at Skillspire to hone my front-end skills.

When I'm not coding or designing, you can find me playing video games, experimental cooking, and reading about human psychology.
