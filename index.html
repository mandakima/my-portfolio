About Me

TL;DR: Second-generation Cambodian American accounting professional pivoting into Front-End Development.

Business Acumen Developer & Tech Advocate for Career Changers

I'm a second-generation Cambodian American and the first in my family to graduate from college. Previously, I worked for Fortune 500 companies including Vail Resorts and Marriott International as an accountant. It wasn't long before I discovered I didn't just want to help business needs, but also design solutions for users!

I completed my UX Design studies through Designlab and Full-Stack Development course through Skillspire to reduce the wall between designers and developers. I am driven to find effective solutions and bring a realistic outlook to every collaborative environment!

When I'm not coding or designing, you can find me playing video games, experimental cooking, reading more on human psychology, and sharing knowledge for career pivoters and college students on how to get into tech.
