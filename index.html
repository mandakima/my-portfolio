About Me

TL;DR: I'm a previous accounting professional pivoting into UI Development.

Hey there!

I'm a second-generation Cambodian American and the first in my family to graduate from college. Previously, I worked for Fortune 500 companies including Vail Resorts and Marriott International as an accountant. It wasn't long before I discovered I didn't just want to help business needs, but also create solutions for users!

I completed my UX Design studies through Designlab and Full-Stack Development course through Skillspire to reduce the wall between designers and developers. I am driven to find effective solutions and bring a realistic outlook to every collaborative environment! In fact, I'm currently volunteering as a frontend developer at Tech Fleet!

When I'm not coding or designing, you can find me playing video games, experimental cooking, and reading about human psychology.
