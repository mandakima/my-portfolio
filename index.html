About Me

I'm a second-generation Cambodian American and the first in my family to graduate from college. In my past life, I was an accountant who worked for some Fortune 500 companies, including Vail Resorts and Marriott International. It wasn't long before I discovered I didn't just want to help business needs, but also create solutions for users.

As a UI Developer, I am driven to find effective solutions and bring a realistic outlook to every collaborative environment. Designlab helped me understand how designers think with users in mind, and I'm currently studying web development at Skillspire to hone my front-end skills.

When I'm not coding or designing, you can find me playing video games, doodling, experimental cooking, and reading about human psychology.

TL;DR: I'm a previous accounting professional pivoting into UI Development.
